About Me

Hello! My name is Liam and I'm a video game composer, film composer, and songwriter from Canada.

I've worked on several student video games, either as thesis projects or game jams, as well as the student animation film, “Ma Mao”. I’ve also published several different portfolio albums that feature a wide range of styles from jazz to cottagecore.

I write mostly orchestral music, with my primary style being cozy and neo-romantic. I also really enjoy expanding my horizons and trying new genres and styles. I love recording live instrumentalists and implementing adaptive music to video games

You can check out some of my work down below as well as on Youtube, Spotify, Apple Music, Soundcloud, and Amazon. ^.^
